What Information is Stored

When you create profiles in Filling Good~, the following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Professional information (work history, education, skills)
  • Website links (LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio)
  • Professional summary and certifications

This information is stored using Chrome's local storage API and remains on your computer. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

How Your Information is Used

Your information is used solely to:

  • Display in the extension's sidebar when you need it
  • Copy to your clipboard when you click copy buttons
  • Export to a JSON file when you choose to backup

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Filling Good~ has no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no telemetry. The extension works entirely on your device.

Data Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, we have nothing to share. Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it to a file.

Data Security

Your data security depends on your device's security. Since all data is stored locally, we recommend:

  • Using a password on your computer
  • Keeping your browser updated
  • Being cautious about who has access to your device

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual profiles using the delete button
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data
  • Clearing Chrome's extension data removes all profiles

Chrome Sync

If you use Chrome Sync, your profile data may sync across your devices that are logged into the same Google account. This is a Chrome feature, not something we control.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the version date above and notify users through the extension update notes.

For questions about this privacy policy or the extension, please visit our Chrome Web Store page and use the support section.
