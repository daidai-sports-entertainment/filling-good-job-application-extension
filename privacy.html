Filling Good~ Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 10, 2025

Key point: Filling Good~ does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. All profiles you create are stored locally on your device via chrome.storage.local.

What data is stored

The extension stores the fields you enter (e.g., contact details, experience, links) locally on your device to make them available in the sidebar.

How it works

  • When you click the toolbar icon, the extension injects a local sidebar on the current tab.
  • When you click “copy”, that specific field is placed on your clipboard. Clipboard contents are not read or stored.
  • The extension does not scrape page content or track your browsing.

Permissions

  • activeTab: Temporary access to the current tab after you click the icon.
  • scripting: Inserts the sidebar’s script and stylesheet.
  • storage: Saves your profiles locally on your device.

No host permissions and no network requests.

Storage scope

Data is saved using chrome.storage.local. It stays on your device and does not sync across devices.

Data sharing

We do not share data with third parties. No data leaves your device.

Retention and control

Data remains until you delete it. You can remove profiles within the extension or uninstall the extension to remove all stored data.

Security

The extension performs all processing locally. Standard Chrome extension isolation and permissions apply.

Children’s privacy

This product is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
